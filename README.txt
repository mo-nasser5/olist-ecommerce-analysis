# Olist E-Commerce Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of the Brazilian Olist E-Commerce dataset. The goal is to explore customer behavior, sales performance, product categories, delivery efficiency, and customer satisfaction to generate valuable business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

The project combines data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and dashboard development using Python and Power BI.

---

## Business Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Evaluate delivery performance and logistics efficiency.
* Analyze customer reviews and satisfaction levels.
* Generate actionable business recommendations.

---

## Dataset Description

The Olist dataset is a public Brazilian e-commerce dataset containing information about:

* Customers
* Orders
* Products
* Sellers
* Payments
* Reviews
* Geolocation data

The dataset consists of multiple related tables that were merged and transformed during the analysis process.

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The analysis focused on:

### Sales Analysis

* Revenue trends over time
* Monthly sales performance
* Order volume analysis

### Product Analysis

* Best-selling categories
* Revenue by product category
* Product popularity

### Customer Analysis

* Customer distribution
* Purchase behavior
* Repeat customer insights

### Delivery Analysis

* Delivery time evaluation
* Shipping performance
* Delayed order analysis

### Review Analysis

* Review score distribution
* Relationship between delivery time and customer satisfaction
* Customer feedback trends

---

## Power BI Dashboard

An interactive dashboard was developed to provide a visual summary of key business metrics.

### Dashboard Features

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Average Review Score
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Product Category Performance
* Customer Insights
* Delivery Performance Metrics

---

## Key Insights

* Certain product categories generated significantly higher revenue than others.
* Customer satisfaction is strongly influenced by delivery performance.
* Sales exhibited seasonal fluctuations throughout the year.
* Delayed deliveries often resulted in lower review scores.
* A small number of categories contributed a large portion of total sales.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Improve logistics operations to reduce delivery delays.
* Increase marketing investment in top-performing categories.
* Monitor customer satisfaction metrics continuously.
* Implement strategies to improve low-performing product segments.
* Enhance customer retention through personalized offers and loyalty programs.
